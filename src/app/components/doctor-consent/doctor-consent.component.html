Doctor Consent Terms

  The hospital  voluntarily agree to participate in this research study titled “Analysis of Malnutrition Screening Strategies to Monitor and Predict Nutrition Requirement for Geriatric Patients ”

  • The hospital has no issue in collecting the dataset for the research purpose The ethical committee approval is issued for this project.
  •  The hospital agrees to collect the required dataset of geriatric patients’ cases of nutrition screening forms with following details 
  1. Patient’s personal details like name, age, gender, height, weight. Informed consent form will be also filled up for the patients.
  2. Nutrition screening form details that include history about diet details. MNA, MUST,NRS2002, NUTRIC Score these forms will be used to know the diet details in last few weeks.

 

  • The data will be solely used for research purpose and publication.
  • I understand that even if I agree to participate now, I can withdraw at any time or refuse to answer any question without any consequences of any kind.
  • I have had the purpose and nature of the study explained to me in writing and verbal explained in language.
  •  I understand the best and I have had the opportunity to ask questions about the study. 
  •  I understand that I will not benefit directly from participating in this research.
  • I agree to submit data through App or Personal health diary 
  • I understand that all information  provided for this study will be treated confidentially.
  • I understand that disguised extracts from my interview may be quoted in conference presentation, paper publication, and dissertation.
  • I understand that if I inform the researcher that I or someone else is at risk of harm they may have to report this to the relevant authorities -they will discuss this with me first but may be required to report with or without my permission. 
  • I understand that signed consent forms and original audio recordings will be retained and accessed with PI at the address specified until the results of their dissertation.
  • I understand that a transcript of my interview in which all identifying information has been removed will be retained  until the results of their dissertation
  • I understand that under freedom of information legalization I am entitled to access the information I have provided at any time while it is in storage as specified above and below.
  • I understand that I am free to contact any of the people involved in the research to seek further clarification and information.

 

Details of researchers:  Assistant Professor (Research scholar), School Of CET, MIT WPU University, Kothrud, Pune.
